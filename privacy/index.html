Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

We take user privacy seriously. Tabib is a safe, private, text-focused health knowledge platform. Public explanations are text-only, and you should never share highly sensitive personal information in public posts. This policy reads clearly in English; Dari (دری) and Pashto (پښتو) versions are available in-app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic account information: name, email, profile photo, user ID.
  • Account role and status: regular user, verified expert, admin, verification status.
  • User-generated content: questions, explanations/comments, reactions, reviews, chat messages, and voice messages (audio you choose to send in chat).
  • Device & fraud-prevention data: device model, manufacturer, platform, and a randomly generated device identifier, used to detect abuse such as fake or duplicate accounts created on the same device.
  • Referral & rewards data: your referral code, who invited you, who you invited, and reward points earned.
  • Payout information: when you request a reward withdrawal, the payout method and the phone/account number you provide.
  • Business / management data: if you create a pharmacy, clinic or laboratory management system, the records you enter (drugs, sales, patients, appointments) and a name/password you set for that system.
  • Technical and usage data: device and app activity, session statistics, notification preferences, and FCM push tokens.
  • Verification data: documents submitted for expert verification and review status.
  • Location data: approximate and precise location, only when you use the Nearby feature (see below).

2. Location Data

Tabib collects your device location (approximate and precise) only when you use the Nearby feature to find healthcare providers. Location is:

  • Collected only when you grant permission and use the feature.
  • Used solely to show nearby results within the app.
  • Not shared with third parties.
  • Stored only temporarily to power your search session.
  • Revocable at any time from your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate core features (Q&A feed, explanations, reactions, reviews, chat).
  • Verify expert accounts and display verified badges.
  • Rank content and experts based on engagement and activity.
  • Improve app performance, reliability and user experience.
  • Send relevant notifications (account updates, engagement alerts, verification updates, new messages).
  • Enforce safety, moderation and anti-abuse measures, including device-based fraud detection.
  • Operate the referral & rewards program and process withdrawal requests.
  • Power optional pharmacy / clinic / laboratory management features you create.
  • Find nearby healthcare places.

4. AI Processing

When you use the Symptom Checker or the Tabib AI assistant, the text you submit is processed to generate a response. AI output is general health guidance to help you understand your situation — it is not a medical diagnosis and is not a substitute for professional care.

5. Referral Program, Rewards & Fraud Prevention

  • You may invite others with your referral code and earn reward points.
  • To keep the program fair, we detect and block abuse such as self-referrals and duplicate accounts on the same device, for which we store a device identifier and basic device model information.
  • When you request a withdrawal, we share the request, your invited-users list and related device information with our administrators for review before any payout.
  • We may decline rewards obtained through fraudulent or abusive activity.
  • Payout details you provide are used solely to send your reward.

6. Private Chat and Visibility

  • Private chat messages are visible only to participants.
  • Public questions and explanations are visible to the community.
  • Role-based access controls apply (for example, only verified experts can post explanations).

7. Data Sharing & Storage

  • Tabib does not sell user data.
  • We do not share personal data with third parties for their independent marketing.
  • We use trusted infrastructure providers (including Firebase) to operate the app securely. Data is encrypted in transit.

8. Security and Data Protection

  • Authentication is required for key actions (posting, reacting, reviewing, chatting).
  • Access controls limit who can read or write specific data.
  • We apply reasonable security measures to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

9. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain your data while your account is active and as needed to provide the service. You can access and update your profile in the app, and you can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

10. Children’s Policy

Tabib is not directed to children. Accounts are intended for users old enough to consent to the processing of their information under local law. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us to have it removed.

11. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data in-app (Profile → Personal Information → Delete Account) or by request. See the dedicated Account & Data Deletion page for full steps and timing.

12. User Responsibilities

  • Do not post highly sensitive personal health or identity information in public areas.
  • Keep your account credentials secure.
  • Use reporting and moderation tools for abusive or harmful content.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact privacy@tabib.af.
