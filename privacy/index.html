Tabib Privacy & Policy 1111

Effective Date: June 6, 2026

We take user privacy seriously. Tabib is designed to provide a safe, private, and text-focused medical knowledge platform. Public explanations are text-only, and users should never share highly sensitive personal information in public posts.

We collect the following types of information from users:

  • Basic Account Information: Name, email, profile photo, user ID
  • Account Role and Status: Regular user, verified expert, admin, verification status
  • User-Generated Content: Questions, explanations/comments, reactions, reviews, chat messages, and voice messages (audio recordings you choose to send in chat)
  • Device & Fraud-Prevention Data: Device model, manufacturer, platform, and a randomly generated device identifier. We use this to detect abuse such as fake or duplicate accounts created on the same device for our referral program.
  • Referral & Rewards Data: Your referral code, who invited you, who you invited, and reward points earned.
  • Payout Information: When you request a reward withdrawal, the payout method and the phone number or account number you provide to receive payment.
  • Business / Management Data: If you create a pharmacy, clinic, or laboratory management system, the records you enter (e.g. drugs, sales, patients, appointments, entries) and a name/password you set for that system.
  • Technical and Usage Data: Device and app activity, session statistics, notification preferences
  • Verification Data: Documents submitted for expert verification, verification review status
  • Location Data: Approximate and precise location, only when you use the Nearby feature (see below).

Location Data

Tabib collects your device's location (approximate and precise) when you use the Nearby feature to find healthcare providers near you. Location is:

  • Collected only when you grant permission and use the feature
  • Used solely to show nearby results within the app
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Stored only temporarily to power your search session
  • Revocable at any time from your device settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate core features (Q&A feed, explanations, reactions, reviews, chat)
  • Verify expert accounts and display verified badges
  • Rank content and experts based on engagement and activity
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Send relevant notifications (account updates, engagement alerts, verification updates, new messages)
  • Enforce safety, moderation, and anti-abuse measures, including device-based fraud detection for the referral program
  • Operate the referral & rewards program and process reward withdrawal requests
  • Power optional pharmacy / clinic / laboratory management features you create
  • Find nearby healthcare places

Referral Program, Rewards & Fraud Prevention

  • You may invite others with your referral code and earn reward points (each referral is worth a fixed number of points that can be withdrawn as Afghani).
  • To keep the program fair, we detect and block abuse — for example, self-referrals and multiple/duplicate accounts created on the same device. For this we store a device identifier and basic device model information.
  • When you request a withdrawal, we share the request, your invited-users list, and related device information with our administrators for review and fraud checks before any payout.
  • We reserve the right to decline rewards obtained through fraudulent or abusive activity.
  • Payout details you provide (phone/account number) are used solely to send your reward.

3. Private Chat and Visibility

  • Private chat messages are visible only to participants.
  • Public questions and explanations are visible to the community.
  • Role-based access controls are applied (for example, only verified experts can post explanations).

4. Data Sharing

  • Tabib does not sell user data.
  • Tabib does not share personal user data with third-party companies or organizations for their independent marketing use.
  • We use trusted infrastructure providers to operate the app securely.

5. Security and Data Protection

  • Authentication is required for key actions (posting, reacting, reviewing, chatting).
  • Data is encrypted in transit.
  • Access controls limit who can read or write specific data.
  • We apply reasonable security measures to reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

6. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

  • Users can request account deletion and associated data deletion at: tabib.af/delete-account
  • Deletion requests are applied immediately after confirmation or approved request.

7. User Responsibilities

  • Do not post highly sensitive personal health or identity information in public areas.
  • Keep your account credentials secure.
  • Use reporting and moderation tools for abusive or harmful content.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact: privacy@tabib.af
