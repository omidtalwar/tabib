Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: February 14, 2026

This page explains how users of Tabib (package tabib.app.af) can request deletion of their account and associated data.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open the Tabib app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Personal Information.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access your account, send a deletion request from your registered email address to privacy@tabib.af with the subject “Delete my account”. We may ask you to confirm ownership of the account before processing.

What is deleted

  • Account profile data (name, email, photo, role status).
  • User-generated content tied to the account (questions, explanations/comments, reactions, saved items, chat data).
  • Verification submission data/documents linked to the account.
  • Notification (FCM) tokens and account-linked settings.

What may be retained

We may retain limited records where required by law, for fraud prevention, or to resolve disputes — for example, minimal transaction or abuse-prevention logs. Such records are kept only as long as necessary and are not used to re-identify you for other purposes.

Timeframe

Tabib applies account deletion immediately after in-app confirmation or an approved email request. Any retained records covered above are removed once they are no longer required.

Contact

For deletion or privacy support: privacy@tabib.af.
