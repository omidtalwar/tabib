Child Safety Standards – Tabib

At Tabib (طبیب), protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users is very important to us. Tabib is a platform where users can ask health questions and review doctors, clinics, and medical services. We strictly prohibit any content that involves the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Tabib has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, user behavior, or activity that promotes or depicts child exploitation is strictly forbidden. If such content is discovered, it will be removed immediately and the responsible account may be permanently banned.

Reporting Harmful Content

Users can report inappropriate or harmful content directly within the Tabib app. All reports are reviewed and investigated, and appropriate action will be taken according to our policies.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Tabib provides users with the ability to report harmful or inappropriate content directly within the app. Reports related to potential child safety concerns are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

Compliance with Child Safety Laws

Tabib complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. If we become aware of content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation, we take immediate action, including removing the content, suspending accounts involved, and reporting the issue to relevant regional or national authorities when required by law.

Cooperation with Authorities

Tabib complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. When necessary, we cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities to address illegal activities involving child exploitation.
