Delete Your Tabib Account

Last updated: February 14, 2026

This page explains how users of Tabib can request account deletion and what data is deleted.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the Tabib app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Personal Information.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

If you cannot access your account, send a deletion request from your registered email to privacy@tabib.af.

What Is Deleted

  • Account profile data (name, email, photo, role status).
  • User-generated content tied to the account (questions, explains/comments, reactions, saved items, chat data).
  • Verification submission data/documents linked to the account.
  • Notification tokens and account-linked settings.

Deletion Timing

Tabib applies account deletion immediately after confirmation or approved email request.

Contact

For deletion or privacy support: privacy@tabib.af
